Project Snapshot

ARC Cloud Consulting designed and co-developed a full-stack logistics management platform for a regional logistics and supply chain company. The web application provides real-time visibility across the entire supply chain — from order intake through delivery confirmation — replacing fragmented manual processes and spreadsheet-based tracking.

Key Focus Areas

  • Real-time shipment tracking with live status updates for customers and operations teams.
  • Inventory management module with low-stock alerts and automated reorder triggers.
  • Route optimization engine to improve delivery efficiency and reduce fuel costs.
  • Third-party carrier API integrations for multi-carrier tracking consolidation.
  • Role-based portal access for drivers, warehouse staff, dispatchers, and management.
  • Azure-hosted with high availability and geo-redundant data storage.
