Overview

Wiman engaged ARC Cloud Consulting to assess their data security posture and implement a structured Data Loss Prevention programme. The organization handled sensitive customer and operational data across multiple Microsoft 365 workloads and needed a systematic approach to prevent data exfiltration, unauthorized sharing, and accidental disclosure.

The project encompassed a full DLP assessment, policy design, implementation, and documentation delivery — ensuring that Wiman's team could sustain and manage the solution independently after handover.

The Challenge

  • Sensitive data scattered across Microsoft 365 — SharePoint, Teams, Exchange, and OneDrive — with no unified visibility.
  • No formal data classification scheme, making it impossible to apply consistent policy controls.
  • Risk of accidental oversharing of confidential documents with external parties.
  • Compliance obligations requiring demonstrable data protection controls.
  • Limited internal IT capacity to design and maintain complex security policies.

The Solution

ARC Cloud Consulting scoped and delivered a phased DLP implementation using Microsoft Purview as the central governance platform. The approach began with a data discovery exercise to understand the types of sensitive information in scope, followed by a classification framework using Purview sensitivity labels.

  • Deployed Microsoft Purview Information Protection with custom sensitivity label taxonomy (Public, Internal, Confidential, Highly Confidential).
  • Configured DLP policies across Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, OneDrive, and Microsoft Teams.
  • Enabled auto-labelling rules to classify documents at rest and in transit without user intervention.
  • Implemented endpoint DLP controls to restrict data movement to removable storage and unauthorized applications.
  • Delivered administrator and end-user documentation covering policy rationale, label usage, and incident response procedures.
  • Conducted a training session for the IT team and key stakeholders on Purview compliance portal management.
